Safe Transit

High-Safety GPS-Enabled Buses

At IDPS Nidagundi, student transit safety is of paramount importance. We manage a premium, modern fleet of air-conditioned school buses traversing a wide radial network of routes throughout Nidagundi and adjoining regions of Bagalkot district.

Every school bus is subjected to a strict regulatory checklist and compliance audit. We have installed modern security technologies including high-definition interior and entry cameras, active GPS trackers, and speed governors to protect our students during their journeys.

  • Real-Time GPS Tracking

    Parents receive specialized app credentials allowing them to track the exact route position and arrival updates in real time.

  • Female Attendants & Helpers

    Every single bus route is accompanied by a trained helper and a dedicated female supervisor to assist young students.

  • Speed Governors & Safety Checks

    Bus speeds are electronically capped at 40 km/h. Regular emergency drills, fire extinguisher checks, and medical kits are standard on all fleets.
