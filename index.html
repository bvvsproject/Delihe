Welcome to IDPS Nidagundi

Nurturing Global Minds, Preserving Integrity

International Delhi Public School (IDPS), Nidagundi, represents a premium hallmark of modern CBSE educational models. We believe in providing an atmosphere that balances conceptual scholastic foundations with progressive sports, technology, values, and cultural integration.

Our state-of-the-art campus, highly qualified teachers, and safe nurturing frameworks empower students to build global confidence while staying connected to traditional values of empathy and leadership.
