CBSE Curriculum Standards

Fostering Rational Thought & Logic

At IDPS Nidagundi, our academic structure follows the guidelines of the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. Our core teaching methodology rejects the primitive frameworks of rote learning. Instead, we implement a project-based inquiry model.

All classrooms are smart technology classrooms, enabling teachers to demonstrate complex concepts in biology, physics, mathematics, and geography via rich 3D animations and interactive software. This ensures high conceptual retention in students and exceptional performance in national-level boards and competitive exams.

Curriculum Levels

Grade-Wise Academic Focus

Click through the divisions to understand specific academic targets, subject combinations, and learning structures.

Pre-Primary Division (Nursery, LKG, UKG)

Our early childhood education program implements an integrated playway framework. We nurture physical, linguistic, cognitive, and social development through hands-on activities, audio-visual storytelling, and Montessori-styled play materials.

Academic Structure & Subjects:

Playway & Phonetics Foundation
Visual Arts & Motor Skill Activities
Basic Mathematical Concepts & Shapes
Rhymes & Stage-Presence Grooming

Primary Division (Grades I to V)

At this stage, we transition from playway learning to highly structured conceptual learning. We emphasize core fundamentals in languages, mathematical calculations, and environmental awareness, integrated with computer education.

Core Curriculum Focus:

English (Reading, Writing, Grammar)
Mathematics (Basic Operations & Geometry)
Environmental Studies (EVS)
Basic Computer & Logical Scratch Coding

Middle School Division (Grades VI to VIII)

Middle school builds scientific, historical, and linguistic intelligence. We introduce specialized branches of science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and social sciences, along with three language options, robotics, and advanced coding.

Core Curriculum Focus:

Integrated Sciences & Lab Experiments
Advanced Algebra & Pre-Geometry
English, Hindi & Kannada / Sanskrit
STEM Learning, Robotics & Coding

Secondary School Division (Grades IX & X)

Rigorous focus on the CBSE National Board Examinations. We train students to master scientific deductions, mathematical proofs, geographical models, and language proficiencies, with extensive test-series and board pattern evaluations.

Core Board Curriculum Focus:

Physics, Chemistry, Biology (CBSE board)
Mathematics (Calculus, Proofs, Stats)
Social Sciences (History, Civics, Eco)
Information Technology & AI Fundamentals

Senior Secondary Division (Grades XI & XII)

Specialized education designed to shape academic careers. We offer high-impact Science and Commerce streams taught by distinguished faculties, integrating national competitive training (JEE / NEET / CA foundation) within CBSE boundaries.

Offered Streams & Subjects:

Science: PCM / PCB (with CS or Biology)
Commerce: Accounts, Business Studies, Eco
Applied Mathematics & Statistics
Entrepreneurship & Physical Education
How We Teach

Teaching Methodologies

Our modern methods ensure every student participates actively and retains complex conceptual designs.

Collaborative Projects

Students work in groups to solve mechanical models or conduct geographical surveys, building team dynamics.

Experiential Labs

Every theoretical science concept is mapped directly to weekly experiments inside sophisticated, modern labs.

Coding Integration

Logical reasoning is honed through dedicated block-coding, Scratch, Python, and robotics programming challenges.

Continuous Feedback

Comprehensive evaluations and personalized parent counseling help target individual academic gaps early.

All-Round Excellence

Co-Curricular & Aesthetic Arts

Academic excellence is incomplete without a healthy mind and creative expression. At IDPS Nidagundi, we mandate active participation in co-curricular clubs, including vocal/instrumental music, classical/contemporary dance, robotics clubs, public speaking, and visual arts.

Our athletic department comprises dedicated physical education teachers coaching students in cricket, swimming, football, karate, yoga, and gymnastics, aiming to establish exceptional bodily intelligence alongside boards success.
