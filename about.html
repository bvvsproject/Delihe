IDPS Legacy

Pioneering Premium Education Standards

Established with a deep commitment to excellence, International Delhi Public School, Nidagundi, has grown to become one of the most respected premium CBSE schools in the region. We pride ourselves on creating an academic framework that stimulates logical deduction, critical reasoning, and deep physical fitness.

Our core pedagogy revolves around active inquiry. We discourage rote learning. By providing state-of-the-art smart learning systems, highly sophisticated physics, chemistry, biology, and robotics laboratories, we prepare our students to lead globally in technological innovation, governance, and business.

At IDPS Nidagundi, education is an adventurous journey. Our campus is built to nurture physical, intellectual, and aesthetic faculties simultaneously, ensuring a highly integrated character for every student.

Our Journey

Milestones of Excellence

Tracing the growth of IDPS Nidagundi from its initial foundation to the premier international campus it is today.

2011

Establishment & Foundation

The school was established with a small cohort of 150 students, focused on establishing fundamental academic standards and safe transport structures.

2015

CBSE Affiliation Obtained

IDPS Nidagundi received its official CBSE Board affiliation, expanding its class structures to Middle and Secondary school divisions (up to Grade 10).

2019

Smart Campus Infrastructure Growth

Built full-scale smart digital classroom facilities, robotics laboratories, modern biology/chemistry labs, and complete campus perimeter CCTV security.

2023

Senior Secondary Expansion & Awards

Introduced Senior Secondary Science and Commerce divisions (Grade 11 & 12). Awarded as the "Most Innovative Premium School" in North Karnataka.
